Lasagna

This classic lasagna recipe is made with an easy meat sauce as the base. Layer the sauce with noodles and cheese, then bake until bubbly! This is great for feeding a big family and freezes well, too.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Tomato Paste
  • Meat

Steps

  1. 2 x 28 oz. cans tomatoes - crushed or whole is best! If you get whole canned tomatoes just crush them in your hands.
  2. 1 pound ground beef, preferably lean
  3. Once they're done, rinse them with cold water using a colander and set them aside. Try to separate them a bit so they stick less later.
